Description

Unfinished is a 2021 memoir by Indian actress Priyanka Chopra. Described as a "collection of personal essays, stories and observations", the book chronicles important moments in Chopra's life and her twenty-year-long career, such as working as an actress-producer and UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador and her marriage to the American singer and actor Nick Jonas. After facing a one-month postponement, it was released by Penguin Random House on 9 February 2021 and reached The New York Times Best Seller list in the United States and topped the Nielsen BookScan best seller list in India.
